ThriftyPiano's Projects

Welcome to my collection of projects! Feel free to contact me through Discord (ThriftyPiano #1905 or @thriftypiano) with any feedback or suggestions. Most of these projects are also available on Khanacademy. Because of compatibility issues, a lot of programs have poor text formatting (however, that doesn't affect how cool they are).

Games

Data Defense

A cyber shooter game with over 30 types of guns and drones, power ups, and more. My largest project ever! 50+ hours of riveting gameplay. I still haven't beat it! Saving implemented through LocalStorage.

Clash Royale v1.1

The final version of my Clash Royale replica , a 2-week turned into 2-year long project. Features terrible graphics, champions with no abilities, spells that glitch half the time, and more!

Fruit Ninja

My replica of fruit ninja that features 4 game modes, 8 swords, and terrible colored-circle fruit graphics. Still has cool particles and gameplay though!

Space Golf

Ever played Angry Birds Space? This is very similar, with planets that you must putt around. Consists of 15 rage-inducing levels.

BLASTER

A nice, short tower-defense game featuring six different turrets that I made in like 2019 (so don't expect it to be high-quality).

Don't Touch Black

A small game that isn't as racist as it sounds. Dodge spikes, lasers, and a spikey blob. You're screwed, but you have a shield.

Fall

A simple 2D version of Run. Mouse to move, click to jump.

Simulations

Marble Machine

Inspired by this site, the marble machine allows you to create tracks, marble races, or even computers (it is supposedly Turing complete).

Rocket Simulator v2.5.7

A fun rocket simulator in which you can shoot for the stars! Has many features (including achievements). Can you find the Easter Egg?

Cloth

Vertlet integration tearable cloth (left click to drag, right click and drag for scissors). Originally had a picture of Oh Noes plastered on it that was removed due to compatibility issues.

Molecules

A satisfying simulator of rainbow molecules bouncing around your screen. Press any key to create molecules and interact with them using your mouse.

Water Park

A water-simulator where you can collect rain and place walls to bounce water around. Unreliable collisions and terribly laggy. Would not recommend.

Wave

My most successful water simulation featuring Vertlet integration and 6.4k nodes.
